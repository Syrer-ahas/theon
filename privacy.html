Privacy & Cookie Policy

Tactical Web uses a small set of technical cookies and measurement tools that are essential for platform security, preserving user sessions, protecting forms from automated abuse, and ensuring reliable operation. These necessary technologies are deployed automatically and enable the site to function correctly.

Data used to improve AI features

With your explicit consent, we may process anonymized inputs, prompt structures, interaction metadata, and feedback to improve our AI models and product quality. This data is used only to refine natural language understanding, reduce bias, and improve contextual relevance. We do not use collected inputs for advertising profiling or share them with third-party marketing networks.

Performance and diagnostics

When enabled, aggregated and pseudonymized telemetry helps us audit performance, identify errors, and understand how people navigate the platform. These analytics are used exclusively for technical improvements and capacity planning.

Security and retention

All data is stored securely with industry-standard encryption where applicable. We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described and allow users to review, adjust, or revoke consent at any time via the privacy controls linked from the footer.

Choices and contact

You can revoke consent at any time by clearing the site cookie or using the site controls. If you have questions about data use or would like to request deletion, contact us at privacy@tacticalweb.example.
